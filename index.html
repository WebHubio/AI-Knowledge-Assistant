⚠️ The Problem

Employees often spend valuable time searching through internal documents, SOPs, technical manuals, and company knowledge. This slows productivity, increases repetitive questions, and delays decision-making.

Slow Information Search

Finding the right document can take several minutes or even hours.

Repeated Questions

Teams repeatedly answer the same questions from different employees.

Reduced Productivity

Employees lose valuable time instead of focusing on important work.

💡 Our Solution

The AI Knowledge Assistant uses the Qwen open-source language model to understand internal company documents and answer employee questions instantly using natural language.

AI Document Search

Search policies, SOPs and manuals instantly.

Natural Language

Ask questions just like chatting with a colleague.

Human Verification

Important responses can always be reviewed by experts.

🚀 Key Benefits

⚡ Faster Information Access

📚 Better Knowledge Sharing

Company knowledge becomes accessible to every department.

⚠️ Risks & Limitations

  • Privacy of confidential company documents.
  • Hallucinations or incorrect AI responses.
  • Integration with existing company systems.
  • Model accuracy depends on document quality.
  • Regular maintenance and updates are required.

✅ Final Recommendation

Deploy the AI Knowledge Assistant as an internal pilot project for one department first. Collect employee feedback, monitor response quality, and gradually expand deployment after successful testing.
